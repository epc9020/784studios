Digital to Analog Conversion

Want the real analog look? We'll take your clean digital audio or video file, record it to a magnetic tape/send it over the air, and digitize it again for a truly dirty, imperfect analog feel!
  • For audio - Put your audio recordings into a tape
    • Type I;Normal tape - Normal, low fidelity tape, could sound muffled at times depending on cassette used.
      99 cents a minute + 30 cents for conversion efforts
    • Type II;CrO2 Tape - Better than Type I tape, sound is more high fidelity.
      $1.10 a minute + 30 cents for conversion efforts
    • Type IV;Metal tape (We currently do not carry this tape) - Exellent sound! The most Hi-Fi you'll get out of an audio cassette!
  • For video - Put your videos into a tape or send them off though the air and back
    • SLP Tape - The lowest video quality from a video cassette, you'll definitively want to be using Hi-Fi audio with this speed unless the idea is to also have bad sound.
      $5.00 for a video of 10 minutes or less, $4.30 every 4-8 minutes afterwards
    • SP Tape - The best sound and video you can get on one of these video cassettes
      $8.50 for a video of 8 minutes or less, $5.40 every 31/2-61/2 afterwards
    • Live Television Broadcast - Send your video over the analog airwaves
      $6.00 for a video of 10 minutes or less, $4 every 10 minutes afterwards
    • Recorded Television Broadcast - Can't miss your favorite broadcast! Record the airwaves to watch it later!
      $11.00 for a video of 8 minutes or less, $7 every 8 minutes afterwards.

You can ask for dissortions through email. Be aware that you will be charged extra for dissortions, since dissorting analog video requires some machine abuse. This extra charge allows us to maintain and repair our equipment.
